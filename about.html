Home·The Club

Built on brick, leather, and decades of fight.

For ten years we've quietly built one of the most respected boxing and strength rooms in Toronto. No mirrors. No music gimmicks. Just the work, the people, and the standards we hold each other to.

The Mission · Round 01

We help you reach your goals and true potential — physically, mentally, emotionally — and create longevity in the gym and in life.

We are a community that cares for one another and champions each other's success. No egos. No image. No fake-it-til-you-make-it. Just the work, the hours, and the people beside you doing the same.

The Story · Round 03

A decade of discipline.

From a 2,000 sq ft room on Bering Avenue to one of Toronto's most decorated training facilities — built one round, one member, one championship banner at a time.

2015 / The Beginning

Open the door.

Stevie Baily and a small group of coaches put a heavy bag on a hardwood floor in Etobicoke and started teaching anyone who walked in. No marketing. No glossy brochures. Just craft, taught honestly.

2018 / The Expansion

Add the floor.

An Eleiko strength & conditioning floor went in next to the bags. Miguel Valdez joined to lead S&C. The room doubled. The standard didn't move.

2021 / The Youth Program

Build the next ten.

Little Warriors® and Youth Boxing launched alongside our proprietary Boxing Band System®. Today, dozens of young athletes age 4-16 train weekly — some for sport, all for the life skills.

2024 / Today

10,000 sq ft.

Cleto Reyes professional boxing equipment. Eleiko platforms. Hot-rock sauna. Smoothie & coffee bar. Eight certified coaches. Hundreds of members from age 4 to 74. One standard: excellence through discipline.
