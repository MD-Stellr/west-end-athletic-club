What's the catch?

There isn't one. But here's what people ask before they sign up — and the honest answers.

What does "no catch" actually mean?

It means we don't ask for a credit card. You don't get auto-enrolled in anything. After your three days, you decide — sign up, walk away, or take more time to think. Whatever you want.

Do I need to bring anything?

Athletic clothes, water bottle, towel. Hand wraps and gloves are provided on your first class — after that, we can recommend a starter set if you decide to stay.

What if I've never boxed before?

Perfect. Most first-timers start in Tech-Fit Boxing or The Boxing Blueprint — no contact, no sparring, just the fundamentals taught at your pace. Your coach will walk you through every move.
