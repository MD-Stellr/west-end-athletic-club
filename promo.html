Common Questions · Round 04

What's the catch?

There isn't one. But here's what people ask before they sign up — and the honest answers.

What does "no catch" actually mean?

It means we don't ask for a credit card. You don't get auto-enrolled in anything. After your three days, you decide — sign up, walk away, or take more time to think. Whatever you want.

Do I need to bring anything?

Athletic clothes, water bottle, towel. Hand wraps and gloves are provided on your first class — after that, we can recommend a starter set if you decide to stay.

What if I've never boxed before?

Perfect. Most first-timers start in Fitness Boxing — no contact, no sparring, just the fundamentals taught at your pace. Your coach will walk you through every move.

Can I use my free pass on weekends?

Yes — your three consecutive days can fall anywhere in the week. Walk in Friday, train Saturday, train Sunday, you're good.

What if I can't make it three days in a row?

The pass is valid for seven days from sign-up, so you have flexibility. Reach out and we'll help you plan it around your schedule.

I'm out of town visiting. Can I use this?

Sorry — the free pass is for new members in the Toronto area. But if you're in town short-term, our drop-in rate is friendly. Reach out and we'll sort you out.
