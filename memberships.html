The fine print, in plain English.

Quick answers to what most people ask before signing up. Still have a question? Hit us up — we'd rather answer it now than have you guess later.

Why don't you list prices?

Because membership pricing changes with promotions, season, and your specific schedule. Reach out — we'll walk you through what fits in two minutes. No high-pressure call. No follow-up spam.

Do I have to sign a long contract?

No. Monthly memberships are month-to-month. Annual founders memberships lock your rate for life — but if life changes, talk to us. We've never trapped anyone in a membership they couldn't use.

What's the difference between a class and open gym?

Classes are coached group sessions — show up and follow the program. Open gym is independent training with access to bags, ring, weights, and machines during posted hours. Most members use a mix of both.

I've never boxed. Will I get my head taken off?

No. Group classes are non-contact unless you opt into sparring. Sparring is invite-only and only happens after your coach decides you're ready — and even then, never to hurt, always to grow.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes — for medical, travel, or work reasons. Just tell us in advance. We'd rather pause it than lose you.

Is there parking?

Yes — free on-site lot at 283 Bering Ave, plus street parking on Bering and Wingold. Bike racks at the front entrance.
