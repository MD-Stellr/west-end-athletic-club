Youth Boxing Program

Powered by West End Athletic Club's Band System®. This non-contact program teaches boxing fundamentals. Beginner Levels 1–3: straight-line and lateral movement, the six basic punches, and basic defense. Advanced Levels 4–6: combination punching with movement and pivots, advanced defense, and counter-punching — a seamless flow of defense to offence. Youth boxers also develop discipline, focus, confidence, commitment, and resilience.
