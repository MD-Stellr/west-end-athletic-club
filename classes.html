Never boxed before? Start here.

  1. 01

    Book any group class

    Show up 15 min early. Wraps are loaned, gloves are loaned. Wear whatever you'd wear to run.

  2. 02

    A coach walks you through it

    Stance, jab, footwork — the basics in the first ten minutes. No one expects you to know anything.

  3. 03

    Use your three free days

    Three consecutive days, every class on the schedule, the bag room, open gym — all in. Decide afterward.
