A boxing gym in Etobicoke, built like a family.

Beginner to champion, ages 4 to 64: one community, one standard, in the heart of Toronto's west end.

West End Athletic Club is a full boxing and fitness gym at 283 Bering Ave in Etobicoke, minutes from Mimico, The Junction, Bloor West Village and High Park. We welcome complete beginners, busy professionals, parents, youth athletes, and competitors — 11 programs and 36 classes a week spanning boxing fundamentals, fitness boxing, strength & conditioning, women's-only classes, youth boxing for ages 4 to 16, sparring, and an amateur fight team.

What sets us apart is who's on the floor with you. Our coaches include a 3-time Canadian Heavyweight Champion, a Team Canada boxer, and a professional who has shared the ring with a pound-for-pound great — real credentials, real coaching, in a room that feels like family. Rated 4.9 stars across 117 Google reviews, with free on-site parking and a full ring, bag floor, strength area, and sauna. It's boxing done right.
